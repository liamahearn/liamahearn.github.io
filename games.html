Games

HyperJustice

HyperJustice is a fast-paced, single-player, third-person shooter akin to GunZ, stylized after retro action movies and PS1 games. I worked closely with a small team of friends to create and publish a game in 90 days, as part of the 90 Days and Ship It! game jam. I served as the main artist, responsible for UI design and pixel art, providing detailed testing feedback, and corresponding with team members remotely.

Tale of Moss

A top-down retro adventure reminiscient of handheld RPGs, Tale of Moss is based on the D&D campaign my friends and I have been playing for the last year. The game revolves around running into our heartstruck characters all around town as a mysterious man named Moss.

Mines of Scoria (Demo)

Mines of Scoria is a turn-based FPS styled after games like DOOM and CULTIC. Manage your resources wisely to clear the winding paths of an ancient dwarven mine with nothing but a lantern and your trusty shotgun! This project is still very much a work in progress, but play around with the first stage and discover the complexities of the Mines of Scoria!

Sewer Panic!

Inspired by classic Game-and-Watch handheld titles, this project seeks to embody a stylistic approach, featuring limited colors and animation. I found that limiting the controls to just the left and right arrow keys mimicks the simple control scheme found today in mobile "endless runner" games- in many ways, the natural evolution of the limited scope of arcade classics.
