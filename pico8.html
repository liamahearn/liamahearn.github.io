Tale of Moss: Legends of Parthenica

A top-down 2D retro adventure, reminiscient of handheld RPGs, based on the D&D campaign my friends and I have been playing for the last several months. The entire party is hopelessly enamored by an NPC named Moss, so the game revolves around running into our heartstruck characters all around town.

(WIP) Demonball Demake

A demake of the 2018 GMTK Game Jam entry Demonball by Room of Snakes, this is a work in progress game that strives to replicate the smooth controls and creative mechanics that the original offers. As of now, enemies are nowhere in sight, so have fun kicking that ball around as fast as it will go!

Game & Watch

A two-button arcade game styled after Nintendo's handheld Game & Watch series, this cartridge is an endless circus act of keeping colored balls bouncing, using the left and right arrow keys, as they pass through a construction site!

Gravity and Elasticity Simulator

A 2D, point based physics simulation with toggleable gravity and elasticity. Click to create a series of points connected by rubber bands that bounce and stretch all over! Like much of my other projects in PICO-8, this demanded smart usage of Lua's array-like structures, tables.

Paint Program

A simple drawing tool, with toggleable pen size, colors, and even a fill tool! This project was an experiment on seeing if I could smooth out the pencil tool positionally between frames, and figure out how to program a fill bucket on my own!
